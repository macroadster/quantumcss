Steve Jobs

Co-founder of Apple Inc., visionary innovator, and pioneer of the personal computer revolution.

Philosophy
"Here's to the crazy ones. The misfits. The rebels. The troublemakers. The round pegs in the square holes. The ones who see things differently."
— Steve Jobs

Journey of Innovation

1976

Apple Founded

Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne founded Apple Computer Company in Jobs' parents' garage.

1984

Macintosh Launch

Introduced the Macintosh, the first commercially successful personal computer with a graphical user interface.

1985

Departure from Apple

After a power struggle, Jobs left Apple and founded NeXT Computer.

1997

Return to Apple

Apple acquired NeXT, bringing Jobs back to the company he co-founded.

2001

iPod Revolution

Launched the iPod, revolutionizing the music industry and introducing iTunes.

2007

iPhone Introduction

Unveiled the iPhone, changing the smartphone industry forever.

2010

iPad Launch

Introduced the iPad, creating a new category of mobile computing devices.

2011

Legacy

Steve Jobs passed away, leaving behind a legacy of innovation and design excellence.
